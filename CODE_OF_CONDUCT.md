# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want UltraFrame AI to be a welcoming and respectful project for users, contributors, translators, testers, and maintainers.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to:

- be respectful and constructive;
- assume good intent when possible;
- keep discussions focused on the project;
- avoid harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language;
- respect privacy and do not publish private information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- abusive, threatening, or harassing comments;
- discriminatory language or imagery;
- sexualized language or unwanted attention;
- trolling, deliberate disruption, or repeated off-topic arguments;
- sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action when behavior harms the project or its community.

If you see a problem, report it through GitHub or through the author's website:

https://dagnaruscode.eu
