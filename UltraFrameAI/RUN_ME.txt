UltraFrame AI

1. Open UltraFrameAI.exe in this folder.
2. Select the folder that contains your video files.
3. Click "Scan files".
4. Click "Start batch".

The app expects ffmpeg.exe, ffprobe.exe, and realesrgan-ncnn-vulkan to stay next to the EXE in the portable package.

If you ship or replace FFmpeg binaries for the native encoder path, use shared LGPL-friendly DLLs only.
Keep them dynamic, avoid GPL/nonfree builds, and include the matching third-party notices in the release package.
